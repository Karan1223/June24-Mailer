Optimise Your Jaw Crusher Performance.

Enhance efficiency with proper feeding practices! Ensure in-line feeding to prevent clogging and choke feeding to maximize capacity and material shape. Avoid over and under-sized feed to maintain consistent productivity. Scalping out fines reduces wear on components and downtime. Manage feed consistency with well-graded materials and limit tramp metal to prevent damage. Avoid lightweight and sticky materials that reduce efficiency.
